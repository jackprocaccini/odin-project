Rice for Neanderthals

Description

A staple of any noobish diet. 1 cup of rice = 2 cups of water.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Aquire white rice
  2. Add 1 cup of white rice to a pot. Make sure you have something to cover the pot with.
  3. Add 2 cups of water
  4. Put a tablespoon of butter into the pot
  5. Cover the pot and set the burner to medium heat (or if you're op cook it on high heat)
  6. Check the pot every few minutes. When all the water is evaporated, scoop out the rice and eat
